Yayo

We started out as friends with big ideas. In around 2011 we started getting together and sharing ideas. At the time we were limited as to how much we could do due to our technical expertise. Over the years as we branch out, gaining experience in fields that would help us realize the ideas that we had all those years we decide to form a company. Yayo Studios is the company that we formed. Our goal is to create product and tools that will help impact others in a meaningful way.

Beljom

Beljom is a classified ad platform/marketplace. The goal of this application is to aid the buying and selling process in Sierra Leone. The cost of setting up a website usually deter businesses from setting up one. It can also be a hassle for buyers to locate a seller. The platform aims to bring the two together eliminating the overhead for the sellers and providing ease for the buyers.
